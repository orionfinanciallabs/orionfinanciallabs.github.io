**What is a Home Loan?**

A home loan, also known as a mortgage, is a loan provided by a bank or a financial institution to a borrower to purchase a house or a plot of land. The borrower is required to repay the loan with interest over a specified period of time, typically 15-30 years.

**Types of Home Loans Available in Hyderabad**

There are several types of home loans available in Hyderabad, catering to different needs and requirements. Some of the most popular types of home loans include:

* **Self-Occupied Home Loan**: This type of loan is designed for individuals who want to purchase a self-occupied residential property.
* **Investment Home Loan**: This type of loan is designed for individuals who want to purchase an investment property, such as a rental property or a holiday home.
* **Construction Loan**: This type of loan is designed for individuals who want to build a house or a commercial property from scratch.
* **Home Improvement Loan**: This type of loan is designed for individuals who want to renovate or improve their existing home.
